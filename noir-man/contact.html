Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unworn, unwashed items with original tags. Items must be returned in their original condition with all packaging. Once we receive your return, it typically takes 5-7 business days to process your refund.

How long does shipping take? ▼

We offer free worldwide shipping on all orders. Standard delivery typically takes 5-10 business days. Express delivery is available for an additional fee. You'll receive tracking information via email once your order ships.

Do you offer custom sizes? ▼

Yes, we offer custom tailoring services on select pieces. Custom orders typically take 4-6 weeks to complete. Please contact our team at hello@noirbrand.com to discuss your specific requirements.

How do I care for my NOIR pieces? ▼

Each piece comes with detailed care instructions. Most NOIR garments are best maintained through professional dry cleaning. Store them on wooden hangers in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. We recommend having them professionally pressed annually to maintain their pristine appearance.

Are NOIR pieces sustainable? ▼

Sustainability is core to our philosophy. We produce in limited quantities, use ethically sourced materials, and partner with certified suppliers. Our pieces are designed to last decades, reducing the need for replacement and minimizing environmental impact over time.
