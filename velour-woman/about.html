Our Story

VELOUR was founded with a simple belief: that luxury fashion should be accessible, sustainable, and above all, effortless. We create pieces that transcend trends, offering timeless elegance for the modern woman.

Each garment is thoughtfully designed and crafted with premium materials, ensuring both quality and comfort. We believe in the power of a well-made piece to transform your wardrobe and your day.

Our journey began with a passion for sustainable fashion and a commitment to supporting artisan craftsmanship. Today, VELOUR stands as a beacon of soft luxury, beloved by women worldwide.

Our Values

Sustainable

We are committed to environmental responsibility in every step of our production process, from sourcing to packaging.

Handcrafted

Our pieces are thoughtfully made by skilled artisans who take pride in creating garments of exceptional quality.

Timeless

We design pieces that transcend seasons and trends, creating lasting wardrobe staples you'll treasure for years.

Inclusive

Beauty comes in all sizes. Our extended size range ensures every woman can find her perfect fit and feel confident.

Meet the Founder

Sarah Mitchell founded VELOUR after years of working in luxury fashion houses across Paris, Milan, and New York. Frustrated by the disconnect between sustainability and high-end design, she set out to prove that you don't have to compromise on either.

Today, VELOUR represents her vision: a brand that celebrates the beauty of simplicity, the power of quality, and the elegance of understated luxury. Every collection tells a story of passion, craftsmanship, and genuine care.
