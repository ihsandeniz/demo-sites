Our Story

Founded in 1892

In the heart of Paris, a master craftsman established the House of LUMIÈRE with a singular vision: to create accessories that transcend time. Each piece was conceived as a wearable work of art, combining exquisite materials with uncompromising craftsmanship.

Over three centuries, LUMIÈRE has remained true to this vision, evolving with the times while honoring our heritage. Our atelier continues the tradition of handmaking each accessory with meticulous attention to detail.

Our Craftsmanship

Handmade by Master Artisans

Every LUMIÈRE piece begins in the hands of our master artisans. With decades of experience and an unwavering commitment to excellence, they work with the finest materials sourced from around the world.

From the selection of raw materials to the final polish, each step is performed with precision. We believe in quality over quantity, which is why every LUMIÈRE accessory is crafted to last generations.

Sustainability Pledge

At LUMIÈRE, luxury and sustainability are not mutually exclusive. We are committed to minimizing our environmental impact while creating pieces that endure.

Ethical Sourcing

We work exclusively with suppliers who share our commitment to ethical practices. All materials are responsibly sourced, and we maintain complete supply chain transparency.

Eco-Conscious Production

Our atelier operates on renewable energy, and we maintain a zero-waste manufacturing process. Every scrap is repurposed or properly recycled.

Durability First

By crafting pieces built to last, we reduce the need for replacement. Our repair and restoration services ensure your LUMIÈRE accessories can be enjoyed for generations.

Community Impact

We invest in our artisan communities, providing fair wages and supporting educational programs. A portion of all sales is donated to environmental initiatives.
