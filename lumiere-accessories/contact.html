Frequently Asked Questions

How long does standard shipping take? ▼

Standard shipping within Europe takes 5-7 business days. International orders typically arrive within 10-14 days. Express options are available at checkout.

What is your return policy? ▼

We offer free returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Return shipping is complimentary with our prepaid label.

Do you offer custom or bespoke pieces? ▼

Yes. Our artisans create custom pieces tailored to your specifications. Contact our VIP services at vip@lumiere.com to discuss your vision.

Are your products authentic? ▼

Absolutely. Every LUMIÈRE piece is handcrafted in our Paris atelier and comes with a certificate of authenticity. We maintain complete supply chain transparency.

Do you offer gift wrapping? ▼

Yes, complimentary luxury gift wrapping is available for all purchases. Personalized gift messages are included at no extra cost.

How should I care for my LUMIÈRE piece? ▼

Each item comes with a detailed care guide. Generally, store in the provided dust bag away from direct sunlight. Use only soft cloths for cleaning. Professional restoration services are available.
