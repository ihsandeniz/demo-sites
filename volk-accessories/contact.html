FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does shipping take? ∨
Standard domestic shipping takes 3-5 business days. International shipping varies by location, typically 7-14 business days. Express shipping (1-2 days) is available for an additional fee.
What is your return policy? ∨
We offer a 30-day return policy. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Return shipping is free for defective items. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving your return.
Is there a warranty? ∨
All VOLK products come with a 5-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and craftsmanship. This does not cover normal wear and tear or accidental damage.
Do you ship internationally? ∨
Yes, we ship to over 15 countries worldwide. International customers may be responsible for customs duties and taxes. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination.
How do I care for my VOLK products? ∨
Each product comes with detailed care instructions. Generally: clean leather with soft cloth, rinse watches under fresh water after salt water exposure, store away from magnetic fields, and keep items in a cool, dry place. Natural patina development is part of the aging process.
